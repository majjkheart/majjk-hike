Why We Walk

When a child is in cardiac crisis, the medical team is focused on saving that child.

But the parents sleeping in hospital chairs for months are largely on their own.

When a child is admitted to the cardiac ICU, the entire family moves in. Jobs pause. Bills pile up. Mental health goes unaddressed. MAJJK was founded by two heart parents who lived it.

Every dollar raised at Heart Hike 2026 supports mental health access, direct financial relief, and research for families of children with congenital heart disease at Phoenix Children's Hospital.
